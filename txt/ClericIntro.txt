In the sacred tapestry of Eldoria, the Cleric emerges as a guiding light.
A new sacred harmony unfolds as you embody the healer, restoring balance to a realm besieged by chaos.
Your journey is one of divine purpose, mending both wounds and destinies.

Divine Aegis - The cleric invokes the power of their deity, creating a protective aura that shields allies from harm.
The Divine Aegis not only absorbs incoming damage but also gradually heals those within its radius.
It's a crucial move for providing support and sustaining the party in challenging encounters.
