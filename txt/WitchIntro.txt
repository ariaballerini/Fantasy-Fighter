Within the mystical tapestry of Eldoria, the Witch emerges as a master of the arcane arts.
Eldritch energies weave through the very fabric of the realm as the Witch, with cauldron in hand,
navigates the delicate dance between power and peril. In a land steeped in ancient magic,
the Witch's journey unfolds, casting spells that shape the destiny of all.

Arcane Tempest - Drawing upon the arcane forces, the witch conjures a tempest of magical energy that rains down upon enemies.
The Arcane Tempest damages and debuffs foes, disrupting their abilities and leaving them vulnerable to further attacks.
This move showcases the witch's mastery over the mystical arts.
