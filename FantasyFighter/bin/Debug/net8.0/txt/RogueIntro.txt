Amidst the shadows of Eldoria, the Rogue emerges as a silent force.
In a realm teeming with secrets, your skills are indispensable.
Navigate the intricate dance of intrigue, execute precision strikes, and unveil the hidden threads that weave through Eldoria.

Shadow Dance - The rogue seamlessly melds with the shadows, becoming nearly invisible to enemies.
During the Shadow Dance, the rogue gains enhanced speed and critical strike chances, allowing for swift,
deadly strikes from unexpected angles. This move is perfect for ambushes and hit-and-run tactics.
