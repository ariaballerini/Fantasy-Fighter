In the untamed heart of Eldoria, the Amazon stands as a primal force. The wild landscapes echo with the call of the untamed,
and the Amazon, with unparalleled strength, roams freely. Every step leaves an indelible mark on the rugged terrain of this realm.
Embark on a journey where the savage ballet of the Amazon shapes the very essence of the wilderness.

Serpent's Wrath - The Amazon taps into the primal energy of the wilderness, unleashing the Serpent's Wrath.
This move transforms her attacks into lightning-fast strikes imbued with poison.
Each successful hit not only deals damage but also inflicts a venomous effect, gradually sapping the life force of the enemy.
