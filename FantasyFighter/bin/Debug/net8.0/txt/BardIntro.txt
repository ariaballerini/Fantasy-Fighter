In Eldoria, where echoes of magic resonate through the air, your journey as a Bard is a melodic adventure,
as you harness the mystical harmony that binds Eldoria together.
Through music and magic, shape the destiny of a realm enraptured by your artistry.

Melody of Distortion - The bard plays a haunting melody that distorts reality, creating illusions and confusion among enemies.
This move disorients foes, causing them to attack each other or miss their strikes.
The Melody of Distortion is a versatile crowd control ability that turns the battlefield into chaos.
